Transact

Fast:
  • Transactions on Nexus confirm within 50 seconds.
Free:
  • It is free to send and receive NXS, tokens and assets (NFTs). However, there is a 0.01 NXS fee applied to every contract generated within 10 seconds of the last transaction, in order to prevent transaction spam attacks.
Secure:
  • Your PIN is requested every time a transaction is created, and unlike other cryptocurrencies, it is not possible to send to an invalid or nonexistent address.
Easy:
  • You can send NXS to usernames or names, replacing the need to copy and paste large Base58 addresses.
Decentralized:
  • Nexus is programmable digital money, meaning contracts can transact tokens and assets (NFTs) without the requirement for third parties such as brokers.
